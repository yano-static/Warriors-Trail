Recover what’s lost — faster

Warrior’s Trail is a student-focused interactive platform for reporting and finding lost items across UE campus. Tag the exact spot on the campus map, add details, and search reported items — all in one place.

Report an Item View Map

Easy reporting

Fill a short form and tap the map to mark the exact location where you lost or found an item.

Campus-limited map

The interactive map is limited to UE campus bounds so students stay focused on relevant areas.

Search & Filters

Quickly filter items by type (phone, wallet, keys, etc.) and search descriptions.

Why this helps

Many items are never reclaimed because there’s no central, searchable system. This platform reduces friction, helps students cooperate, and improves the chance items are reunited with their owners.
