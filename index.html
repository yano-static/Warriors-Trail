What is Warrior’s Trail?

Warrior’s Trail is the official interactive lost and found map for the University of the East Manila campus. It helps students and staff locate, report, and claim lost or found belongings—all safely within UE’s campus boundaries.

How It Works

  • Search for lost items on the Interactive Campus Map.
  • Report a lost or found item by pinning the exact location within the campus.
  • Contact owners or finders securely.
