Problem

Students often misplace personal belongings on campus and struggle to recover them due to the lack of a centralized, user-friendly lost and found system. Traditional methods like handing items to security or posting on bulletin boards are commonly ignored or overlooked.

Innovation

Warrior’s Trail provides an interactive platform where students can submit lost items by entering a description and tagging the exact spot on a campus map. The system offers a searchable database to find or report items, increasing recovery chances and encouraging cooperation.

HCI Angle

This system emphasizes Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) by focusing on simple forms, intuitive map interactions, search filters, and clear item categorization. Map icons, legends, and notifications enhance accessibility and usability for students.
