Our Mission

To empower the UE Manila community to easily recover lost belongings, connect finders and owners, and keep the campus safer and friendlier for everyone.

How does it work?

  • Anyone can report or search for lost and found items on campus.
  • All reports are limited to on-campus locations, ensuring privacy and trust.

  • Text-to-Speech feature for accessibility and hands-free information access.

Features

  • Interactive Map: Visual representation of lost and found items across campus
  • Real-time Updates: Powered by Firebase Realtime Database
  • Search & Filter: Quickly find specific items by type or keywords
  • Text-to-Speech: Accessibility feature using Web Speech API
  • Mobile-Friendly: Responsive design works on all devices
